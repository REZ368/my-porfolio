SAED (System Analytics and Evaluation Development)

Is a personal JAVA project for TESDA developed to manage and monitor fruit inventory efficiently. The system is designed to handle the basic inventory tasks such as recording terms, tracking incoming fruit supplies, managing pricing, and monitoring quantities.

Technologies used: PHP, HTML, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, Mysql

Users:
Purchase Request & Order Management :Allows administrators to record, update, and track supply requests made by different departments as it monitored for creations that tied to approved PRs.
Inventory Monitoring: Keeps track of item quantity, item history, and supply flow in and out of the system.
Admin-Only Access: Designed specifically for internal use by authorized personnel handling supply operations.

SAED is built with simplicity, efficiency, and usability in mind. Tailored for administrative use, it empowers internal staff to handle supply processes accurately and efficiently without relying on complex third-party platforms.
