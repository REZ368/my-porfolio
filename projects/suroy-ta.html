Suroy-Ta! Travel Blog Capstone Project

Suroy-Ta! is a travel blog focused on posting and sharing content about Cebu's hidden gems and well-known tourist destinations. The main goal of the website is to promote Cebu as a travel destination by allowing users to share their experiences and explore new places.

Technologies used: Vue.js for the front-end, Tailwind CSS for styling, Node.js for the back-end, and MySQL for the database.

The platform allows users to share experiences and explore places in Cebu, promoting both famous destinations and hidden gems.

Users:
Customer: Can post travel experiences, update profiles, like/comment, and report posts.
Promoter: Verified users who promote businesses or spots through posts.
Guest: Can browse content and maps but cannot interact deeply.

Overall, Suroy-Ta! aims to be a helpful and engaging platform for travelers, local businesses, and tourism enthusiasts. By connecting people through shared travel stories and verified promotional posts, the website not only promotes Cebu's famous spots but also uncovers its hidden gems. Whether you're exploring for fun, sharing your adventures, or showcasing your business, Suroy-Ta! creates a space where the beauty of Cebu can be experienced and appreciated by everyone.
